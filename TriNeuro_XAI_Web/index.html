Decision Support

Radiologist Review Required

TriNeuro AI provides decision-support outputs only. The final interpretation should be based on the complete MRI study, prior examinations, clinical context, and radiologist assessment.

Evidence Generated

  • Classification No-tumor/tumor screening and glioma/meningioma routing when a tumor is detected.
  • Segmentation Lesion mask used for volume, diameter, axial span, laterality, and shape estimates.
  • Explainability Grad-CAM++ and guided attention panels show regions that influenced the model.
  • Report draft Structured report text is generated from model outputs and segmentation measurements.

Review Priorities

  • Confirm MRI sequence identity and alignment before relying on overlays.
  • Compare segmentation boundaries against original T1c, T2w, and FLAIR images.
  • Check whether Grad-CAM focus is anatomically plausible and overlaps the lesion region.
  • Correlate automated tumor type with imaging phenotype, prior studies, and pathology when available.

Report Safety Notes

Automated measurements are estimates and may change with modality quality, registration, field of view, or incomplete uploads.

No tumor-screening result should exclude subtle pathology without full image review.

The generated report should be edited before use in any clinical, educational, or presentation context.
